PART IV (DIRECTIVE PRINCIPLES OF STATE POLICY) >>

Article 45. Provision for early childhood care and education to children below the age of six years

  1. 1[The State shall endeavour to provide early childhood care and education for all children until they complete the age of six years.]
  1. [1] Subs. by the Constitution (Eighty-sixth Amendment) Act, 2002, s. 3 for art. 45 (w.e.f 1-4-2010).
