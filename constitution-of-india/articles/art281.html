PART XII (FINANCE, PROPERTY, CONTRACTS AND SUITS) >> CHAPTER I. FINANCE >> Distribution of Revenues between the Union and the States

Article 281. Recommendations of the Finance Commission

  1. The President shall cause every recommendation made by the Finance Commission under the provisions of this Constitution together with an explanatory memorandum as to the action taken thereon to be laid before each House of Parliament.
