PART XIV (SERVICES UNDER THE UNION AND THE STATES) >> CHAPTER II.—PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSIONS >>

Article 318. Power to make regulations as to conditions of service of members and staff of the Commission

  1. In the case of the Union Commission or a Joint Commission, the President and, in the case of a State Commission, the Governor 1of the State may by regulations—
    1. (a) determine the number of members of the Commission and their conditions of service; and
    2. (b) make provision with respect to the number of members of the staff of the Commission and their conditions of service:
  2. Provided that the conditions of service of a member of a Public Service Commission shall not be varied to his disadvantage after his appointment.
  1. [1] The words “or Rajpramukh” omitted by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956, s. 29 and Sch. (w.e.f. 1-11-1956).
