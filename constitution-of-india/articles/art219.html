PART VI (THE STATES) >> CHAPTER V. -THE HIGH COURTS IN THE STATES >>

Article 219. Oath or affirmation by Judges of High Courts

  1. Every person appointed to be a Judge of a High Court 1shall, before he enters upon his office, make and subscribe before the Governor of the State, or some person appointed in that behalf by him, an oath or affirmation according to the form set out for the purpose in the Third Schedule.
  1. [1] The words “in a State” omitted by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956, s. 29 and Sch. (w.e.f. 1-11-1956).
