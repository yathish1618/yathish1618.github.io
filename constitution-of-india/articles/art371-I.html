PART XXI (TEMPORARY, TRANSITIONAL AND SPECIAL PROVISIONS) >>

Article 371-I. Special provision with respect to the State of Goa

  1. 1[Notwithstanding anything in this Constitution, the Legislative Assembly of the State of Goa shall consist of not less than thirty members.]
  1. [1] Ins. by the Constitution (Fifty-sixth Amendment) Act, 1987, s. 2 (w.e.f. 30-5-1987).
