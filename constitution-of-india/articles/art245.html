PART XI (RELATIONS BETWEEN THE UNION AND THE STATES) >> CHAPTER I. LEGISLATIVE RELATIONS >> Distribution of Legislative Powers

Article 245. Extent of laws made by Parliament and by the Legislatures of States

  1. (1) Subject to the provisions of this Constitution, Parliament may make laws for the whole or any part of the territory of India, and the Legislature of a State may make laws for the whole or any part of the State.
  2. (2) No law made by Parliament shall be deemed to be invalid on the ground that it would have extraterritorial operation.
