1THE CONSTITUTION (APPLICATION TO JAMMU AND KASHMIR) ORDER, 1954
  1. C.O. 48
  2. In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of article 370 of the Constitution, the President, with the concurrence of the Government of the State of Jammu and Kashmir, is pleased to make the following Order:—
  3. 1. (1) This Order may be called the Constitution (Application to Jammu and Kashmir) Order, 1954.
  4. (2) It shall come into force on the fourteenth day of May, 1954, and shall thereupon supersede the Constitution (Application to Jammu and Kashmir) Order, 1950.
