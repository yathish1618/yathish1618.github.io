PART XIV (SERVICES UNDER THE UNION AND THE STATES) >> CHAPTER I. SERVICES >>

Article 308. Interpretation

  1. In this Part, unless the context otherwise requires, the expression “State”1[does not include the State of Jammu and Kashmir.]
  1. [1] Subs. by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956, s. 29 and Sch., for “means a State specified in Part A or Part B of the First Schedule” (w.e.f. 1-11-1956).
