PART VI (THE STATES) >> CHAPTER I. GENERAL >>

Article 152. Definition

  1. In this Part, unless the context otherwise requires, the expression “State”1[does not include the State of Jammu and Kashmir].
  1. [1] Subs by ibid., for “means a State specified in Part A of the first Schedule”.
