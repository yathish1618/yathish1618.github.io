PART IV (DIRECTIVE PRINCIPLES OF STATE POLICY) >>

Article 48A. Protection and improvement of environment and safeguarding of forests and wild life

  1. 1[The State shall endeavour to protect and improve the environment and to safeguard the forests and wild life of the country.]
  1. [1] Ins. by the Constitution (Forty-second Amendment) Act, 1976, s. 10 (w.e.f. 3-1-1977).
