PART XVII (OFFICIAL LANGUAGE) >> CHAPTER IV. SPECIAL DIRECTIVES >>

Article 350. Language to be used in representations for redress of grievances

  1. Every person shall be entitled to submit a representation for the redress of any grievance to any officer or authority of the Union or a State in any of the languages used in the Union or in the State, as the case may be.
