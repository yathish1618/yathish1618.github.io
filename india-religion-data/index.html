Here we primarily focus on population by religious communities based on the data released by Census of India.

Population

Of course we should begin with the population distribution across states. The map on left shows population density across states. U.P is still leading the race!

The total population growth of india, in fact, is mildly decreasing over decades. At its peak, it was almost 25% in '70s. Now in 2011, the population growth fell to about 18%.

As ever, thanfully, the gender ratio is not too skewed.

The rural demographic hasn't changed too much either. In 2001, it made up about 72% and now its still 68%.
